You will play this game based on luck, you will move randomly ranging from -4 to 4. You will encounter a lot of symbols that you can play with. They will determine your scores by which you need to be careful. The tables belows shows the score equivalent.

NAME SYMBOL SCORE
PUMPKIN B 2
WIZARD W 3
BROOM S 4
DEMON D -5

If you press QUIT , you will be able to see how many points you got from your luck. But when you have BAD LUCK , all of your points will be taken as well as you are taken out from the game.
